What is ToffeeShare?
We are a free and independent peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing service from
the Netherlands that prioritizes your privacy and keeps your data safe. We
store nothing online: simply close your browser to stop sending.
Our mission is to make sure people keep their data safely into their own
hands, as it should be.
+
Files are shared straight from your
device
When you close the browser tab your files are no longer
accessible, minimising the risk of anyone getting
unwanted access.
ToffeeShare uses the peer-to-peer technology WebRTC
to find the shortest path, meaning sometimes your data
doesn't even have to leave the building!
No more file size limits
Because we don't store the data, there's no need for file
size limits. Just share files of any size or whatever
amount. As long as you keep an eye on your own data
usage.
